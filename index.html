Ti

is the symbol of titanium,
the 22nd chemical element on the periodic table
in the Universe we're living in.

Ti

is also a homo sapiens,
who is not so sapient
as his Species name suggested.

Ti

drinks coffee more than tea,
likes savory foods more than sweet,
but for alcohol he would take anything from beer to whiskey.

Ti

hasn't achieved much in his life,
and now in a PhD program he's trying to survive,
wishing the knowledge and wisdom would thrive.
