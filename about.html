Ti

is the symbol of titanium,
the 22nd chemical element on the periodic table
in the Universe we're living in.

Ti

is also a homo sapiens,
who is, luckily, sapient enough
to pursue a lifestyle with science.

Ti

drinks coffee more than tea,
eats plant-based foods more than meat,
yet for alcohol he'd take anything from beer to whiskey.

Ti

hasn't achieved much in his life,
but with every rock and mountain he climbed,
the knowledge and wisdom would thrive.
